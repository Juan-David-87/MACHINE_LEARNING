Machine Learning Use Case

Product Recommendation System in an E-commerce Environment

Business Problem Statement

An e-commerce company similar to Amazon or Mercado Libre faces several challenges due to the expansion of its product catalog and changing customer behavior.

Current Problems:

  • Thousands of products available in the catalog.
  • Customers struggle to quickly find what they want.
  • Low conversion rate.
  • High cart abandonment.
  • Low customer retention.
  • Generic recommendations such as “best-selling products”.

Negative Business Impact:

  • Lower sales revenue.
  • Reduced average order value.
  • Loss of customers to competitors.
  • Poorly personalized user experience.

Relationship with Machine Learning

This problem can be addressed by implementing a Machine Learning-based Recommendation System. The system analyzes large volumes of user behavior data and identifies hidden patterns. Based on these patterns, the model predicts which products are most likely to interest each individual customer.

  1. Detects patterns not visible through traditional analysis.
  2. Provides personalized recommendations at an individual level.
  3. Continuously improves as new data becomes available.

How Does It Work in Practice?

The system collects data about user behavior, such as viewed products, purchases, time spent on pages, and visited categories. The model analyzes relationships between users and products to generate personalized predictions. When the customer revisits the platform, the system displays real-time recommendations based on their previous activity and similar user behaviors, increasing the probability of purchase.

What Does the Model Learn?

The model learns user behavior patterns and relationships between products. It identifies individual preferences such as favorite categories, preferred price ranges, and brands. It also detects products frequently purchased together and factors influencing buying decisions. Over time, it anticipates user interests even before a specific search is made.

How Does It Improve the Company?

A recommendation system increases conversion rates and average order value by presenting relevant products to each customer. It enhances user experience by simplifying navigation and reducing search time, leading to higher satisfaction and loyalty. As a result, the company increases revenue, strengthens customer retention, and gains a competitive advantage.
